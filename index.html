Equipment: Single kettlebell (moderate to heavy weight)

Focus: Back, biceps, shoulders, triceps, core, and balance

Duration: 40 minutes

Structure: Flow warm-up ‚Üí 2 strength components ‚Üí core/balance cool-down

Upper Body Flow Warm-Up 8 minutes

Complete 3 rounds, flowing smoothly between exercises

  1. KB Halo - 5 reps each direction
    Wake up shoulders and thoracic spine
  2. Alternating Bottoms Up KB Clean - 8 reps total
    Activate grip and shoulder stability
  3. KB Arm Bar - 3 reps each side
    Shoulder mobility and stability
  4. KB Push-Up - 6-8 reps
    Hands on KB handle, activate chest and triceps
  5. KB Bent Over Row - 8 reps each arm
    Light weight, activate posterior chain

Rest 45 seconds between rounds

Strength Component 1: Back & Biceps 12 minutes

Superset A (3-4 sets)
  1. Gorilla Row - 6-8 reps per arm
    30X1 tempo (3 sec down, 0 pause, explosive up, 1 sec pause)
  2. KB Horn Grip Bicep Curl - 8-10 reps
    20X0 tempo (2 sec down, controlled up)

Rest 30 seconds between exercises, 90 seconds between sets

Superset B (3-4 sets)
  1. KB Chainsaw Row - 6-8 reps per arm
    20X0 tempo
  2. Half Kneeling KB Eccentric Bicep Curl - 5-6 reps per arm
    50X0 tempo (5 sec eccentric focus)

Rest 30 seconds between exercises, 90 seconds between sets

Strength Component 2: Shoulders & Triceps 12 minutes

Superset C (3-4 sets)
  1. Seated KB See-Saw Press - 6-8 reps per arm
    30X1 tempo
  2. Tall Kneeling KB Overhead Tricep Extension - 8-10 reps
    30X0 tempo

Rest 30 seconds between exercises, 90 seconds between sets

Superset D (3-4 sets)
  1. Half Kneeling KB Press - 5-6 reps per arm
    20X0 tempo
  2. KB Crush Grip Floor Press - 8-10 reps
    21X1 tempo
    Squeeze KB sides, focus on tricep and inner chest

Rest 30 seconds between exercises, 90 seconds between sets

Core & Balance Cool-Down 8 minutes

Complete 2-3 rounds, focusing on quality and control

  1. KB Turkish Sit-Up - 3-4 reps per arm
    Slow and controlled, focus on core stability
  2. KB Windmill - 3-4 reps per side
    Light weight, focus on mobility and balance
  3. Bottoms Up KB Carry - 20 steps per arm
    Challenge grip, core, and shoulder stability
  4. KB Hollow Body Pull Over - 6-8 reps
    Slow and controlled, focus on lat stretch
  5. KB Horn Grip Russian Twist - 8 reps per side
    Controlled rotation, engage obliques

Rest 60-90 seconds between rounds

Workout Guidelines:

  • Weight Selection: Use moderate weight for warm-up, heavier for strength work, lighter for cool-down
  • Tempo Focus: Follow prescribed tempos for maximum benefit - they control intensity
  • Rest Periods: Stick to rest times to maintain proper stimulus and recovery
  • Form First: Quality over quantity - reduce weight if form breaks down
  • Progression: Add weight or reps weekly, focus on one variable at a time
